Life At Campus

Life At PGC

At Punjab Colleges, a vast range of cultural, educational,sports and social activities are available and involved in campus life is quickest way to become a part of the college community and to create one's own enhanced learning experience. Campus life activites are built around the concept of encouraging each combination of these activites create a pleasant student life on campus for our students. Getting student community member to express his/her own talents and to respect all members of our campus community. Along with high academic standards and opportunities for development in a wide variety of fields, the quality of our faclities is also a major reason why Punjab Colleges are a top choice of for students across Pakistan

Seminars & Workshops

In order to provide experiential knowledge to our students, we organize seminars and workshops with industry professionals and motivated speakers from all walks of life. Students benefit from real life experiences and success stories.

Cultural Festival & Fun Fairs

Every year we organize fun filled cultural festivals where students get a chance to exhibit their artistic potential.

Concerts

From time to time PGC organizes concerts with renowned muscicians and singers in all our compuses. We stongly believe in promoting the art and culture of our country.

Welcome And Farewell Party

From the time our students join us till they leave, we try our best to give them an experience of a lifetime. We celebrate these memorable moments of fun and joy with our students each year.

Study And Recreational Trips

PGC organizes trips to exciting cultural and northern destinations for all our students. The trips are governed by rules and supervised by our experienced staff

Sports Gala

Sports Gala is an annual sports event where all students get to participate and compete with each other. The winners are recognized by PGC and duly rewarded while giving them an opportunity to compete for national level clubs.

Science & Art Exhibitions

Every year at PGC, we organize Sciene and Arts Exhibitions so that our students get a chance to pursue their passion interest in life. This generates a platform for innovation and art.
