Associative Degree in Arts

B.A.

Introduction

This programme is designed to ensure that all students must have the basic knowledge that symbolizes an educated person. This degree provides different opportunities and explores different fields that suit to the students. We offer the following subjects:

ADA (Part-I and Part-II)

Compulsory Subjects

  1. English
  2. Islamic Studies
  3. Pakistan Studies

Elective Subjects Offered

  1. Education
  2. Physical Education
  3. Pol-Science
  4. Journalism
  5. Punjabi
  6. English Literature

Optional Subjects Offered

  1. Persian
  2. Islamic Studies

Duration

The duration of the programme is two years. The University conducts terminal examination for 1st year and 2nd year separately.
