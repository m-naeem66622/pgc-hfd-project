Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Q: What is the minimum requirement for enrolment in FSc (Pre-Engineering)?

Ans: Minimum requirement is matriculation or equivalent. For further details, visit your nearest campus.

Q: What are the total marks of FSc (Pre-Engineering)?

Ans: FSc is a two-year programme in parts with 550 marks each year. Thus total marks of FSc are 1100

Q: What are the subject-wise marks for FSc (Pre-Engineering)?

Ans: In FSc (Pre-Engineering), you study 6 subjects in both parts 1 & 2. The subject wise breakdown of marks for FSc Pre-Medical is as follows:

  • English (Compulsory) = 100 Marks
  • Urdu (Compulsory) = 100 Marks
  • Islamic Education (Compulsory) = 50 marks (Part I only)/ Pakistan Studies (Compulsory) = 50 marks (Part II only)
  • Physics = 100 marks
  • Chemistry = 100 marks
  • Mathematics = 100 marks
The same subjects are studied in FSc Pre-Medical part II.

Q: Which fields are open to pursuing after FSc Pre-Engineering?

Ans: After completing your FSc Pre-Engineering, you can choose from a wide range of career choices whichever attracts you the most. Examples include:

  • Aeronautical engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Industrial engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Business Manager and more
