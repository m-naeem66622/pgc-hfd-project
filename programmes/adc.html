Associative Degree in Commerce

B.Com

Introduction

ADC (IT) is a comprehensive knowledge of commerce and IT. It helps the students become business professionals. They can serve better in the fields of Industry and Trade. We offer the following subjects:

ADC (IT Part-I)

  • Business Statistics & Mathematics
  • Computer applications
  • Economics
  • Financial Accounting
  • Functional English
  • Introduction to Business
  • Money, Banking and Finance
  • Islamic Studies

ADC (IT Part-II)

  • Advance Financial Accounting
  • Auditing
  • Business Communication & Report Writing
  • Business Law
  • Business Taxation
  • Cost Accounting
  • Economics of Pakistan
  • Pakistan Studies

Duration

The duration of the programme is two years. The University conducts terminal examination for 1st year and 2nd year separately.
