Introduction

FA General Science programme is designed for students who are interested in developing careers in the fields of science. The programme develops a rich understanding of both compulsory and elective subject with the help of an in-depth curriculum and an experienced teaching faculty. The subjects are as follows:

Subjects FA (Part-I and Part-II)

Compulsory Subjects

  1. Urdu
  2. English
  3. Islamic Studies
  4. Pakistan Studies

Elective Subjects

  1. Physics
  2. Mathematics
  3. Statistics
  4. Computer Science
  5. Economics

Duration

The duration of the programme is two years. The BISE conducts terminal examinations for 1st year and 2nd year separately.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Q: What is the minimum requirement for enrolment in FA?

Ans: Minimum requirement is matriculation or equivalent. For further details, visit your nearest campus.

Q: What are the total marks of FA?

Ans: FA is a two-year programme in parts with 550 marks each year. Thus total marks of FA are 1100

Q: What are the subject-wise marks for FA?

Ans: In FA, you study 6 subjects in both parts I & II. Both parts carry equal marks that are 550. The subject wise breakdown of marks for FA is as follows:

  • English (Compulsory) = 100 Marks
  • Urdu (Compulsory) = 100 Marks
  • Islamic Education (Compulsory) = 50 marks (Part I only)/ Pakistan Studies (Compulsory) = 50 marks (Part 2 only)
  • Elective Subject 1 = 100 marks
  • Elective Subject 2 = 100 marks
  • Elective Subject 3 = 100 marks
The same subjects are studied in FA part II.

Q: Which fields are open to pursuing after FA?

Ans: There are numerous avenues to choose from after FA General Science. Fields include (but not limited to):

  • Computer Science
  • Journalism
  • Economics
  • Psychology
  • Law (LLB)
  • Business Administration
  • Mass Communication/Marketing
  • Sale and much more
